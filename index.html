Design Is More Than Appearance

1) Proportion Over Decoration

Good design rarely depends on excess detail. Balance, spacing, and proportion often communicate quality more effectively than ornamentation.

2) Materials Tell a Story

Texture, weight, and finish affect how an object feels in daily use. Thoughtful material selection builds trust without explanation.

3) Consistency Builds Identity

A clear visual language repeated over time becomes recognisable. Consistency is not limitation—it is focus.
